About Me

history_edu My Journey and Focus

I am currently pursuing my Master's in Artificial Intelligence, building upon the strong hardware and automation foundation I developed during my B.Tech in Automation and Robotics at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham.

My fascination with how machines perceive their environment drove me to specialize in computer vision in robotics, machine learning, and deep learning.

A robot is just metal, wires, an empty shell of steel and circuits; it moves, but it cannot understand. AI becomes the intelligence within the system, giving the machine the ability to learn, adapt, and make decisions. That's where ordinary machines evolve into intelligent robots.

My passion lies in that exact intersection, where ordinary hardware evolves into intelligent, autonomous systems and I am currently transitioning into an AI and Data Science role with a hyper-focus on Intelligent Robotics.
